Shillong is a hill station in northeast India and capital of the state of Meghalaya. Its known for the manicured gardens at Lady Hydari Park. Nearby, Wards Lake is surrounded by walking trails. North, the Don Bosco Centre for Indigenous Cultures features displays on the regions native people. Waterfalls include the Elephant Falls to the southwest.
